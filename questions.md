# Comprehensive Directory of Coach Check-In Questions

This document is a compiled directory of professional coaching questions aggregated from top athletic, bodybuilding, powerlifting, and functional wellness check-in sheets globally. It is designed to act as an open registry for the **Self-Coach** web application, allowing you to select and configure custom inquiries.

---

## 1. Quantitative Readiness & Biometrics (Hard Vitals)
*These metrics are standard numerical or rating inputs (1-10 Scale or Raw Metrics) used to establish baseline statistical averages and trends.*

### Physiological Vitals (Physical Status)
- **Morning Body Weight**: What is your waking weight today (or your 7-day average)? (Lbs or Kg)
- **Heart Rate Variability (HRV)**: RMSSD value captured upon waking. (ms)
- **Resting Heart Rate (RHR)**: Nocturnal or waking heart rate baseline. (BPM)
- **Systolic/Diastolic Blood Pressure**: Standard blood pressure reading. (mmHg)
- **Waking Fasting Blood Glucose**: Waking blood sugar level. (mg/dL)

### Subjective Wellness Pillars (1-10 Scale)
- **Restorative Sleep**: 1 = Terrible, fitful sleep; 10 = Deep, unbroken, fully restorative sleep.
- **Systemic Soreness (DOMS)**: 1 = Complete muscular exhaustion/debilitating pain; 10 = Zero soreness, fully responsive tissue.
- **Joint Fluidity & Pain**: 1 = Extreme joint inflammation/stiffness; 10 = Fluid movement, pain-free.
- **Mental Energy & Focus**: 1 = Heavy brain fog/executive dysfunction; 10 = Elite focus, sharp cognition.
- **Subjective Training Vigor (Desire to Train)**: 1 = Dread training/total CNS drain; 10 = Biting at the bit to load the bar.
- **Digestive Efficiency (Gut Comfort)**: 1 = Severe bloating, cramps, or irregularity; 10 = Perfect digestion, comfortable, regular.
- **Overall Life Stress (Cortisol Load)**: 1 = Overwhelming stress/anxiety; 10 = High calm, in control, relaxed.

## 2. Bodybuilding & Physique Prep Inquiries
*Curated for bodybuilding, physique competitors, and aesthetic reconstruction clients where tissue preservation, fluid balance, and muscle symmetry are critical.*

### Nutrition & Macro Adherence
- **Strict Nutritional Compliance**: "What percentage of your target macronutrients (protein/carbohydrates/fats) did you hit this week? (e.g. 100%, 95%, 85%)"
- **Accidental Free Meals or Deviations**: "Did any unmeasured food, liquids, or restaurant meals slip into your chart? Detail what it was, why it occurred, and your physiological response."
- **Gastrointestinal & Bloat Audit**: "Are you experiencing systemic bloating, gas, reflux, or changes in bowel regularity? If yes, pin down what foods or timing might have triggered it."
- **Hunger and Satiety Window**: "How is your hunger scaling throughout the day? List specific hours where appetite spikes or drops."

### Physique & Aesthetics Check
- **Hydration Depth & Water Intake**: "Did you hit your precise daily fluid targets? Rate your muscle fullness and vascularity versus flat/stringy appearance."
- **Visual Tightness / Fluid Retention**: "Do you feel like you are holding visual subcutaneous water (blurring definition), or does your physique look dry, hard, and detailed?"
- **Progress Photo Notes**: "Note any changes you see in key target areas (midsection, glutes, shoulders, hamstrings) compared to last week."

## 3. Powerlifting & Performance Strength Inquiries
*Designed for strength athletes focusing on progressive overload, nervous system fatigue, biomechanical safety, and bar velocity.*

### Neuromuscular Vigor & Load Tolerance
- **Pacing & Bar Speed Accuracy**: "Did your warm-up lifts and working sets feel heavy and sluggish, or did they possess excellent bar velocity and explosiveness?"
- **RPE Accuracy (Expected vs. Actual)**: "Did your assigned RPE targets (Rate of Perceived Exertion) match the actual difficulty, or did workloads feel significantly heavier than predicted?"
- **Technical Integrity & Lift Mechanics**: "Did you experience form breakdowns or mechanical faults during key lifts (squat, bench, deadlift)? Detail what felt unstable."
- **Central Nervous System (CNS) Vigor**: "How is your grip strength, reaction speed, and general coordination? Any shaking, trembling, or persistent post-workout lethargy?"
- **Nagging Micro-Trauma & Structural Asymmetry**: "Are there any joint pains, ligament twinges, or left/right imbalances creeping up? Define the location and duration."

## 4. Endurance & Cardiovascular Training Inquiries
*Geared toward runners, cyclists, triathletes, and hybrid endurance athletes tracking cardiovascular adaptation and pacing.*

### Cardiovascular States & Aerobic Drift
- **Aerobic Drift & Heart Rate Coupling**: "Did your heart rate spike abnormally at a steady-state power/pace, or did it couple nicely with workload intensity?"
- **Respiratory Comfort (Breathing Rhythm)**: "Assess your breathing efficiency. Did your lungs feel open and deep, or did you experience shallow respiration/early dyspnea?"
- **Hydration & Electrolyte Sweating Rate**: "Did you experience muscle cramping, heavy salt rings on clothes, or sudden bonking during your long endurance sessions?"
- **Footwear, Friction & Skin Safety**: "Are you dealing with hot spots, blisters, saddle sores, or musculoskeletal shin/tendon stress?"

## 5. Mental Drive, Executive Functions & Wellness Inquiries
*Focused on lifestyle, emotional load, behavioral compliance, and self-belief.*

### Mindset & Vigor
- **The "Wins" Ledger**: "What was your absolute biggest victory (physical, mental, nutritional) this week? Reflect on why it succeeded."
- **Emotional Exhaustion / Burnout**: "How is your patience, mood stability, and general enthusiasm? Are you feeling symptoms of psychological training burnout?"
- **Behavioral Slippage & Obstacles**: "What environmental factors, work timelines, or personal events threatened your routine? How can we pivot to insulate against them next time?"
- **Primary Leverage Priority (The Bottleneck)**: "If you could change exactly one execution habit for next week to speed up your transformation, what is it?"
